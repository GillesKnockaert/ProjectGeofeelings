Let's get started

1. Get yourself registered

Start by registering your account. You can also chose not to register but then not all features will be available

2. Show others how you feel

After registering, you can show others yours emotional status by clicking one of the smileys who resemble to your emotional status

Almost there

3. Fill out the form

After you picked a smiley fill out the form. Others wanne know why you are happy or not happy. When you fill in the form, you will also be able to add your location to it.

4. connect with others

Now that you sucesfully shared your status, connect with others so your status gets noticed. When you added youre friends you can even start a conversation with them

One last step

5. Take a look at the map

When you take a look at the map, you can see all the other peaple that use geofeelings. Apply filters to show only your friends or to narrow the map down to radius of your choise.

6. Explore for yourself

There is a lot more to explore so find out for yourself. and don’t forget to tell your friends If you read this, you are ready to jump into action Go to the full site and explore what else you can do.
